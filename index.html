Auto Log for BB10

Powerful trip computer.

Use Auto Log to track fuel efficiency, keep service records, and journal your road trips. Create fill events to record station fills, service events for vehicle service, and waypoints to track your location.

You can also take photographs of anything you record. Use your photos to identify and remember your travels.

The Pro version allows you to log multiple vehicles, and includes powerful data import and export features.

Photo Assistant for BB10

The photographer's companion.

Journal your photography: Record the details of your photo shoots, including date, location, details, and a cover shot. Save and export GPS coordinates to use with photos from non-GPS enabled cameras (requires desktop photo management software to change photos).

Calculate the depth of field for your photographic images: you can describe your camera and lens, and find out exactly how much of your scene will be in focus.

Determine the golden hour: use your location to determine when the magical "golden hour" occurs in the morning and evening, that special time of day when the light is just right.

Photologe for PlayBook

Display a clock with pictures behind it.
  •

Photo + horloge (clock): Display a slideshow of your photos overlaid with a configurable digital clock. A practical use for your tablet when it's docked at your desk or bedside table.

Photologe shows the date and time, in your choice of language and format, with options for position and appearance. It can display photos taken with the tablet's camera or copied from your digital camera or the Internet.
