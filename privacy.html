Privacy Policy

Effective on 2013-07-22 (updated)

This privacy statement describes how One Busy Monkey collects and uses the personal information you provide. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information.

Location services

Some of our applications make use of location-based data. If you allow these services, they will collect information about the Wi-fi routers closest to you and the cell IDs of the towers closest to you.

We use this information to provide the requested location-based service, not to identify you. You will be asked for your permission for the use of location-based services.

Collection and use

Some of our apps collect the following personal information from you:

  • Device identifying information
  • Usage activity about how you interact with our apps, such as screens visited, actions performed, and quantity of data.

We use this information to:

  • Display content based upon your interests
  • Deliver targeted advertisements (ad-supported apps only)
  • Improve the quality of our apps

The content of data that you create or enter is not collected.

Sharing your information

We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

We may share collected information anonymously with third party partners to provide services such as advertising, analytics, and in-app purchases. These third parties are authorized to use this information only as necessary to provide these services to us.

We may disclose your personal information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process.
  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • if One Busy Monkey is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information.
  • to any other third party with your prior consent to do so.

Tracking and ads

We may partner with a third party ad network to either display advertising on our App or to manage our advertising on other sites. Our ad network partner uses technologies to collect non-personally identifiable information about your activities on this and other Apps to provide you targeted advertising based upon your interests.

We may partner with a third party analytics service to report anonymous aggregate usage patterns and demographics for our application. This data will be used by One Busy Monkey to improve the quality of our apps.
